Memento Mori

  • Elegance and passion that leaves an unforgettable trail

On turbulent seas, an inescapably transient pirate ship braves black waters, leagues deep in mystery. Black Phantom “Memento Mori” reveals its hidden treasures to the curious nose in waves.

Woodsy perfumes in all their forms - from sandalwood to cedarwood, from patchouli to Vetyver - but always with a Kilian twist: rum, coffee, cacao, cognac, whisky, as a reminder of Kilian Hennessy's heritage. A lot of attention is taken in each KILIAN refillable perfume bottle, to make them real precious objects. The perfume bottles of the cellars family are adorned with a distinctive black lacquer and meticulously engraved on each side, with the representation of the Achilles shield.

A deadly bite of cyanide accord hides menacingly, like the smile from death itself. Smile back as sugar cane and dark, creamy sandalwood assures a trying journey holds a sweet reward.



The perfume Black Phantom - "MEMENTO MORI" is part of the KILIAN Cellars family.

The perfume bottles of the cellars family are adorned with a distinctive black lacquer and meticulously engraved on each side, with the representation of the Achilles shield.

These details bring to the KILIAN creation a guarantee of luxury, that should not be ephemeral, but should last a lifetime.
